The Problem

Open-source software (OSS) is now ubiquitous, forming a solid digital infrastructure for our everyday life. This digital infrastructure requires tremendous efforts to develop and maintain. Without constant support, destructive consequences such as The Heartbleed Bug could occur. Because of this, maintaining OSS is undoubtedly crucial. However, several obstacles hinder OSS’s sustainability. One major obstacle is the inadequate diversity, especially in gender, which causes a subsequent unwelcoming culture.

OUR MISSION This website presents a census on gender diversity, one of the many diversity measures, in the OSS community. Through this site, you can find the following information:

The general trend of gender distribution in OSS as a whole

A decomposition of the entire population into ecosystems

A longitudinal analysis to reflect how women's participation has changed with time

A comprehensive report of statistics on core vs. all (tentative wording)
